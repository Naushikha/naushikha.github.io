About

Me.

Everything about computers fascinate me; from hardware to software, and everything in-between. This also means that I am always eager to learn and try new things in the field of computing.

The starting point for everything was in 2008; when I managed to get a copy of Visual Basic 6.0 in the laptop that my family shared. What drew me into programming was trying to make my own games (which I did btw; check my projects). With that passion I have got to the point where I am now doing a Bachelors' in Computer Science at University of Colombo School of Computing. But programming is not the only thing that I do. I love listening to Metal music and playing them on my guitar! Also I love to play games, lots and lots of games. My current favorite is 'Mortal Kombat 9'!
If I am to suggest you to listen to one great Metal song and play one great game, it would be Hope Leaves by Opeth and Indigo Prophecy

Feel free to contact me for anything (official/non-official), just drop in a hello and I will reply back. I am big on getting to know new people, so you are absolutely welcome to knock on my door (unless you are a terrorist :P).
