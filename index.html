About

Me.

Doing the impossible is a bold statement. But that's just what I do; imagining a whole new range of possibilities.

My story starts back in 2008; a curious kid, a family-shared laptop and a borrowed copy of Visual Basic 6.0, starting with a burning desire to make the next big thing. A decade and many projects later- challenging the world and aspiring to change it for the better; one line-of-code at a time.
You can find me at my place cranking up metal music and coding away to the beat.

I love meeting new people and working on exciting projects. Let's connect. Drop in a hello.
